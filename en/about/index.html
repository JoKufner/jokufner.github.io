About Me

Freelancer & Coach for the Microsoft Power Platform – from process consulting and Dataverse modeling to Power Apps, Power Automate, and Power BI.
October 1, 2025 · 3 min · Jonas Kufner | Translations:
  • De

Profile Summary#

I help companies use the Microsoft Power Platform to move measurably faster from idea to production – pragmatic, structured, and close to real business processes. My background combines business administration and IT project work (freelance since 2010) with project and test management.
Since July 2018, I have also been working as a project manager at GSD mbH. In the past, I co-developed the web-to-print platform myTriPic.

My approach: making complex tasks easy to use – with a clean data model, iterative methods, and reliable operations.

What I Offer#

  • Power Platform Consulting & Delivery

    • Dataverse modeling, Model-Driven & Canvas Apps
    • Power Automate for end-to-end processes & integration
    • Power BI for self-service analytics & KPI dashboards
    • Governance, environments, ALM, pipelines, testing, and release processes

  • Coaching & Enablement

    • Building internal capabilities (Citizen Dev & Pro Dev)
    • Best practices, solution design, architecture reviews
    • Hands-on sessions and code walkthroughs

  • Process & ERP-related Topics

    • Finance & core processes, compliance & risk analysis
    • Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central (incl. Dataverse integration)
    • Knowledge management & sustainable documentation

Working Approach#

Empirical & Iterative. I break down requirements into clear objectives (e.g., as user stories), deliver early usable increments, and ensure quality through test concepts, automation, and reviews.
Pragmatic. Solutions must have an impact – not just be “correct.”
Sustainable. Knowledge is documented in a way that benefits both operations and follow-up projects.

Key Focus Areas (Selection)#

  • Microsoft Power Platform: Dataverse, Power Apps, Power Automate, Power BI
  • M365 & SharePoint as platform components
  • Dynamics 365 Business Central / Microsoft Dynamics NAV
  • Test management (ISTQB® Foundation) & quality assurance
  • Project management according to IPMA/GPM (Level D)
  • Web & integration technologies (e.g., Node-RED, REST APIs)
  • Data & reporting (SQL, modeling, KPI design)

Certifications & Education#

  • Project Management Professional (GPM)® / IPMA Level D
  • ISTQB® Certified Tester (Foundation Level)
  • Diploma in Business Administration (University of Applied Sciences) (focus: Controlling & IT)

Project Insights Since 2018 (Anonymized)#

  • Adult Education: Dataverse-based system for training planning & evaluation including Model-Driven App, ALM, tests, and master data interface.
  • Consulting Services: Implementation of lean finance and core processes, internal M365 enablement, automation with Power Platform, regular risk reporting.
  • E-Commerce: Implementation of Business Central, migration of master and transactional data, process definition, testing & operational support.
  • Industry & Construction: Data preparation for consolidation, compliance checks, process maps, integrated process visualization including web API & role model.
  • Public Sector: Functional specifications, risk analysis & testing within regulatory frameworks and portal solutions.
  • Web-to-Print: myTriPic – concept, automation, and testing of a platform for personalized products.

Typical Results#

  • Noticeably shorter lead times through automated workflows
  • Transparent data models instead of uncontrolled Excel files
  • Accepted apps, as business departments are involved early
  • Reliable releases thanks to ALM pipelines and test strategy

I am actively developing my role as a freelancer and coach for the Microsoft Power Platform and look forward to exciting new projects – from the initial process idea to a productive solution.
